Privacy Policy

At Inkredible, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, contact details, and medical history when you book an appointment or contact us. We may also collect information about your tattoos and preferences to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used to provide our services, manage your appointments, and communicate with you about upcoming sessions, promotions, and aftercare instructions. We may also use your information to send you newsletters and offers, but only with your consent.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or when we work with trusted partners to provide our services. These partners are obligated to keep your information confidential.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Our website uses secure encryption methods to protect your data during transactions.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your user experience, analyze website traffic, and deliver personalized content. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@inkredible.com.
